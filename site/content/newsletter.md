## Daily Economic Data Digest

Receive Nova Scotia economic data releases in your inbox every business day at 09:30 ET.

Each email includes a summary of the day's Statistics Canada releases relevant to Nova Scotia, with links to full analysis on this site.

**Coming soon.** The newsletter is currently in development. Check back for subscription details.

## What you'll receive

- **Daily digest** — Summary of all releases published that day
- **Key numbers** — CPI, unemployment rate, GDP, and other headline indicators
- **Nova Scotia focus** — National comparisons with a provincial lens
- **Revision alerts** — Notifications when Statistics Canada revises previously published data
